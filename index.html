Sharkie’s Adventure: Game Instructions

1. Move Sharkie

Use the arrow keys to move Sharkie through the underwater world.

2. Sharkie’s Attacks

  • Fin Slap: A strong fin attack that can be used against pufferfish.
  • Bubble Trap: A bubble-based attack that traps and disables jellyfish.

3. Defeating Enemies

  • Pufferfish can only be defeated using Fin Slap.
  • Jellyfish can only be defeated using Bubble Trap.

4. Collect Poison Bottles

Find and collect poison bottles to boost your Bubble Trap. They will make your bubble attack stronger and more effective.

5. Save Poison Bottles for the Final Boss

Don’t use all your poison bottles too early! Save them for the final boss to increase your chances of victory.

6. The Final Boss

The final boss can only be defeated using Bubble Trap.

Poisoned bubbles are twice as effective against the final boss, so make sure to collect enough poison bottles to maximize your attack power!

7. Collect Coins

Collect coins scattered throughout the game to beat the high score and become the best player in Sharkie’s world!
